## 4. Financial Methodology: Median Operational Failure Risk

### Replacing Marketing Claims with Engineering Risk Analysis

**Before** (Undefensible guarantee):
> "$2.1M in Incident Avoidance"

**After** (Forensic risk methodology):
> **Median Operational Failure Risk: $2.1M**
>
> This figure represents a **median operational failure cost**, not a guaranteed ROI. Calculated as the intersection of:
> - **Daily NPT**: $500K/day (North Sea benchmark for jack-up/semi-sub operations)
> - **Median Duration**: 4.2 days (typical fishing/sidetrack operation for depth-related failures)
> - **Catastrophic Liability**: Wellbore integrity failure regulatory penalties + environmental exposure
>
> **Disclaimer**: Actual risk varies by asset, water depth, reservoir pressure, and operational context. This metric represents industry benchmark median, not client-specific guarantee.

### Why This Approach Is Defensible:

1. **Industry Benchmarks**: $500K/day NPT is verifiable North Sea standard
2. **Median vs. Average**: Uses conservative median (50th percentile) not optimistic average
3. **Explicitly Not a Guarantee**: Clear disclaimer about contextual variation
4. **Traceable Methodology**: Can reference industry reports (Wood Mackenzie, Rystad Energy)
