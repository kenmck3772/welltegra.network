FAQ

Do we need to replace our current tools?

No. We read from your existing stack and keep your systems of record as the source of truth.

What happens if our data is messy?

That’s normal. QC rules highlight gaps; we track “data debt” so you can fix the few things that matter.

Can the crew use this offline?

Yes. Runbooks and deviation tags work offline and sync when connectivity returns.

How do you calculate ROI?

We multiply baseline NPT cost by reduction achieved and add optional rework savings. All assumptions are editable.
