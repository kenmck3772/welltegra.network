      Security & Data Protection

      Enterprise-grade controls keep well programs, Gemini prompts, and live operational telemetry secured from the planning office to the rig floor.

      Defense-in-Depth Architecture

      • Zero Trust perimeter. Planner and Mobile Communicator sessions are brokered with short-lived OAuth tokens and conditional access tied to rig site geofences.
      • Encryption everywhere. TLS 1.3 for all traffic, FIPS 140-2 validated KMS for data at rest, and client-side sealing on every Management of Change approval.
      • Separation of duties. Immutable audit trails on Gemini prompt edits, procedure generation, and supervisor sign-off prevent unauthorized workflow changes.

      Data Governance Pipeline

      Identity
      Enterprise Managed Users with SCIM lifecycle management ensure only certified personnel access active wells.
      Segmentation
      Program data, AI embeddings, and live operations streams reside in isolated tenants with per-operator encryption domains.
      Integrity
      Automated hash stamping on every import plus dual attestation before procedures are released to the field.
      Retention
      Configurable retention schedules with defensible deletion aligned to regional regulation and contract terms.

      Response & Assurance

      • 24/7 managed detection with playbooks mapped to rig cyber incidents and cross-domain API anomalies.
      • Quarterly tabletop drills that include Mobile Communicator escalation paths and connectivity failover.
      • Exportable compliance packets covering SOC 2 Type II, ISO/IEC 27001, and NIS2 readiness for operator audits.

      How to use the Mobile Communicator Step-by-step

      The communicator mirrors the latest saved programme and lets authorised supervisors review change requests, evidence, and impact before releasing work.

      1. Open the channel. Tap the Mobile Communicator badge in the planner header. Pending items are flagged in amber.
      2. Select a request. Choose a card in the left column to load the justification, plan snapshot, and decision trail.
      3. Validate the context. Check risk badges, first execution steps, and the on-call crew in the plan snapshot. Review evidence attachments and watchers if escalation is required.
      4. Approve or reject. Enter your WellTegra email and secure PIN, add an optional comment for the log, then submit a decision. A digital seal is generated and the audit feed updates instantly.

      All approvals are stored locally for this demo build. In production, the communicator would synchronise with the WellTegra control plane over an encrypted channel.
