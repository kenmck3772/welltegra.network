Planner Module

Smarter Well Planning

The Planner connects to existing databases like WellView and SAP, scanning historical data for similar wells, identifying risk triggers, and generating pre-populated programs that incorporate lessons learned.

  • Imports planning data from legacy formats (CSV, PDF, WellView XML)
  • Highlights recurring risks using mistake-mining AI
  • Suggests design optimizations based on historic outcomes

Performer Module

Live Operational Intelligence

The Performer connects to WITSML feeds, analyzing live operational data in real-time. It transforms chaotic sensor data into meaningful visual metrics, alerting field teams to anomalies before they escalate.

  • Simulated live data feed from rig sensors
  • Instant KPI visualization and deviation alerts
  • Supports remote operations and mobile interfaces
