## üé• Video Recording Tips (If Creating Recorded Demo)

### Technical Setup

**Software:** Loom, OBS Studio, Camtasia, or ScreenFlow

**Settings:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Audio: 192 kbps or higher
- Webcam: Optional (face in corner adds personality)

**Environment:**
- Quiet room (no background noise)
- Good lighting (if using webcam)
- Stable internet (if uploading to cloud)
- Test recording first (check audio levels)

### Recording Best Practices

1. **Script Your Narration** - Don't wing it, use this script
2. **Slow Down** - Speak 10-15% slower than normal conversation
3. **Pause Between Sections** - Gives viewers time to absorb
4. **Use Cursor Highlights** - Circle or highlight key elements (Loom does this automatically)
5. **Edit Mistakes** - It's okay to re-record segments
6. **Add Captions** - YouTube auto-captions or manual (accessibility + SEO)
7. **Include CTA at End** - "Book a demo at welltegra.com/demo"

### Post-Production Checklist

- [ ] Trim dead air at start/end
- [ ] Add intro title card (3 seconds): "Well-Tegra 5-Minute Demo"
- [ ] Add chapter markers (Act 1, Act 2, etc.)
- [ ] Add outro with contact info
