Our Playbook

The WellTegra Methodology

A systematic approach to transforming subsurface uncertainty into defensible capital decisions. Four phases that take you from data chaos to decision confidence.

  • 01 Diagnose
  • 02 Architect
  • 03 Execute
  • 04 Defend
1

Phase 1

Diagnose - Data, Governance & Uncertainty

We begin by understanding your decision context, data landscape, and organizational challenges. This phase establishes the foundation for defensible decisions.

Key Activities:

  • → Decision workflow mapping and bottleneck identification
  • → Data architecture audit and quality assessment
  • → Stakeholder alignment and incentive analysis
  • → Uncertainty quantification and risk profiling

Phase Outcomes:

  • Clear decision roadmap with identified quick wins
  • Data quality scorecard and improvement plan
  • Stakeholder map with influence/interest matrix
  • Risk register with mitigation strategies
2

Phase 2

Architect - Models, Workflows & Incentives

Design the decision architecture that transforms data into insight. This phase creates the models and workflows that support repeatable, transparent decisions.

Key Activities:

  • → Decision framework design with clear governance rules
  • → Analytical model selection and validation
  • → Workflow automation and tool integration
  • → Incentive structure alignment and KPI definition

Phase Outcomes:

  • Decision governance framework with RACI matrix
  • Validated analytical models with uncertainty bounds
  • Automated workflow documentation and SOPs
  • Aligned incentive structure with balanced scorecard
3

Phase 3

Execute - Tools + Adoption

Deploy the decision architecture and drive adoption across the organization. This phase focuses on execution excellence and capability building.

Key Activities:

  • → Tool deployment and system integration
  • → Team training and certification program
  • → Pilot execution on live decision opportunities
  • → Change management and communication plan

Phase Outcomes:

  • Fully integrated decision support environment
  • Certified team of decision champions
  • Proven pilot results with quantified ROI
  • Organization-wide change readiness assessment
4

Phase 4

Defend - Auditability & Repeatability

Establish audit trails and continuous improvement mechanisms. This phase ensures decisions remain defensible and the system evolves with your needs.

Key Activities:

  • → Decision audit trail implementation
  • → Performance monitoring and KPI dashboards
  • → Lessons learned capture and best practice library
  • → Continuous improvement framework deployment

Phase Outcomes:

  • Complete auditability with decision lineage tracking
  • Real-time performance visibility and alerting
  • Living knowledge base with searchable insights
  • Self-sustaining improvement culture
