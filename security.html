Privacy Policy

Portfolio & Demonstration Site

WellTegra Network and the Brahan Engine are portfolio demonstration projects. This is not a commercial product collecting user data at scale. This site exists to showcase technical capabilities and domain expertise.

Key Principle: We don't collect, store, or process any personal information unless you explicitly contact us via email or schedule a call through Calendly.

What We Don't Collect

This site does NOT use:

  • Cookies (except those required by external services like Calendly)
  • Analytics tracking (no Google Analytics, no user behavior tracking)
  • User accounts or login systems
  • Forms that collect personal information (except demo request buttons)
  • Third-party advertising or tracking pixels

What Happens When You Contact Us

If you choose to contact us via:

  • Email (contact@welltegra.network): Your email address and message content are stored in standard email systems (Gmail). This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.
  • Calendly scheduling: Calendly may collect your name and email to facilitate scheduling. Review Calendly's Privacy Policy for details.
  • LinkedIn: Any connections or messages via LinkedIn are governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

External Links

This site contains links to external services (GitHub, LinkedIn, YouTube, Calendly). When you click these links, you leave WellTegra Network and are subject to the privacy policies of those platforms.

Security Practices

Infrastructure Security

The Brahan Engine backend is deployed on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security:

  • Google Cloud Run: Serverless containers with automatic HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firestore: Managed NoSQL database with encryption at rest and in transit
  • BigQuery: Analytics warehouse with Google-managed encryption keys
  • IAM Controls: Principle of least privilege for service accounts
  • Secret Manager: API keys and credentials stored in Google Secret Manager (never in code)

Development Security

  • Version Control: Public GitHub repository with transparent code review
  • Dependency Management: Regular updates and security scanning via Dependabot
  • No Hardcoded Secrets: All sensitive credentials use environment variables or Secret Manager
  • HTTPS Enforcement: All site traffic uses HTTPS (GitHub Pages enforces this)

Demonstration Data

All well data, toolstring runs, and operational scenarios are synthetic or publicly available examples.

  • No proprietary or confidential industry data is used
  • Well names (e.g., "Well-666") are fictional
  • Equipment catalogs use generic manufacturer specifications
  • Team member profiles are fictional composites for demonstration purposes
Important: This is a portfolio project, not a production system for live operations. Do not submit confidential well data or proprietary information through demo forms or contact mechanisms.

Responsible Disclosure

Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability in the WellTegra Network site or the Brahan Engine, please report it responsibly:

  • Email: contact@welltegra.network with subject line "Security Issue"
  • Include: Description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and potential impact
  • Do not: Publicly disclose the issue before allowing time for remediation

We will respond to security reports within 48 hours and work to address valid issues promptly.

Your Rights

Data Access & Deletion

If you have contacted us via email or Calendly and wish to:

  • Access your data: We can provide copies of email correspondence upon request
  • Delete your data: Email contact@welltegra.network and we will remove your information from our records
  • Correct information: Email us with corrections to any information we hold

Since we don't actively collect user data through the site itself, there is minimal information to access or delete unless you've contacted us directly.

International Users

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (US-based servers) and uses Google Cloud Platform (multi-region). If you are accessing from the European Union or other jurisdictions with specific data protection laws:

  • By contacting us, you consent to data transfer to the United States
  • We respect GDPR principles even though this is a portfolio site, not a commercial service
  • You have rights under GDPR to access, correct, or delete your personal information

Changes to This Policy

This Security & Privacy page may be updated periodically to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision.

Significant changes will be noted on this page. As this is a portfolio site with minimal data collection, changes are expected to be rare.
