## Executive Summary

### Problem Statement
Production casing (9.625" P110) at 8,500 ft MD experienced severe ovalization reducing the internal diameter from 8.681" to 7.1". This restriction prevented passage of standard 2.313" OD intervention tools, severely limiting production optimization capabilities.

### Root Cause
- **Primary Cause:** Reservoir depletion + geomechanical stress
- **Contributing Factors:**
  - 10+ years of continuous production
  - Reservoir pressure decline from 12,500 psi to 8,200 psi
  - Formation loading on casing string
  - Casing positioned at geological stress transition zone

### Solution Selected
**Enventure ESeal 60-ft Expandable Steel Patch**
- **Manufacturer:** Halliburton Enventure
- **Model:** ESeal 60ft x 9 5/8"
- **Serial Number:** ENV-PATCH-23-4421
- **Setting Depth:** 8,500 ft MD
- **Restored ID:** 8.5" (from 7.1")
- **Result:** Full-bore tool access restored

### Business Impact
- **Cost:** $500K (patch + installation)
- **Alternative Cost:** $15-25M (sidetrack or workover)
- **Cost Savings:** $14.5-24.5M
- **ROI:** 29-49x
- **Production Impact:** Full capacity restored
- **Well Life Extension:** 10+ years
