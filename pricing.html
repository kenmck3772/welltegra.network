Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start with a free trial? ▼
Yes! Professional and Team tiers include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. The Planner tier is free forever.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▼
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll pro-rate your billing accordingly.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers (wire/ACH), and purchase orders for Enterprise customers.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), regular security audits, and comply with SOC 2, GDPR, and industry standards. Your data never leaves our secure servers unless you explicitly share it.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼
You can export all your data before canceling. We retain your data for 90 days after cancellation in case you change your mind, then permanently delete it per your request.
Do you offer custom enterprise solutions? ▼
Yes! Our Enterprise tier includes custom integrations, on-premise deployment options, dedicated support, and tailored features. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.
What's included in the API access? ▼
Our REST API provides programmatic access to wells, surveys, plans, and real-time data. Rate limits vary by tier. Full documentation and code examples are available in our developer portal.
Can I get a refund? ▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
