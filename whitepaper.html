WellTegra White Paper

Version October 14, 2025 — A practical loop for well delivery: Plan → Perform → Analyze.

Executive Summary

WellTegra aligns planning, field execution, and analysis around one secure data pool. No rip-and-replace: we reuse your existing systems and apply focused AI where it pays off.

The Problem

  • Siloed tools and retyping across WellView, Sapphire, ERP/PO
  • Inconsistent formats and lost lessons
  • Connectivity constraints and ad-hoc reports

The WellTegra Loop

Planner

~70% prefill of WBS, risks, budget lines from similar wells; edit what's unique.

Performer

Clear offline runbook; one-tap deviation capture; standardized shift reports.

Analyzer

Repeating patterns, KPIs (NPT, cost variance), transparent ROI.

Data Pool & Integration

We map your sources into a governed pool with catalog, quality rules, and lineage. Typical connectors: WellView, Sapphire, ERP/PO, files (PDF/images), time/depth streams.

Focused AI & Security

AI assists with plan prefills, deviation tagging, and pattern surfacing. Per-client isolation with SSO/RBAC, encryption in transit/at rest, and audit logs.

Implementation & ROI

  1. Intake (days 1–5): mapping & QC
  2. Pilot (days 6–14): Planner/Performer live; Analyzer baseline
  3. Review (days 15–20): ROI snapshot and next steps

Commercials

  • Pilot: 2 weeks, fixed price, up to 10 wells
  • Team: 1–2 assets (P+P+A)
  • Enterprise: SSO/RBAC/residency

FAQs

Do we replace systems? No. We orchestrate across them.

Data security? SSO, RBAC, encryption, and isolation by design.

Time to value? Weeks, via a focused pilot.
