# Well‑Tegra PoC — 6‑Week One‑Pager
**Objective:** Prove value without platform changes, using a sidecar approach to ingest vendor exports and generate explainable recommendations.

## Scope
- 1 asset, 10–20 historical interventions, 1 upcoming candidate well.
- Read‑only; no system write‑backs during PoC.

## Deliverables
- Clean PoC dataset; recommendation stubs in **Planner**; NPT breakdown and Lessons Learned in **Analyzer**.
- 3–5 “mistake‑mined” playbooks and a quantified ROI range.

## Plan (Weeks)
1. **Data handshake** (inventory, schema, S3/email path)  
2. **ETL + QA** (parse, map, QC, timelines)  
3. **Mistake‑mining v1** (cluster top failures, costs)  
4. **Planner** (confidence + drivers; TFA baselines)  
5. **Analyzer** (NPT donut; deltas; lessons)  
6. **Read‑out** (KPIs, savings, phase‑2)

## Success Criteria
- ≥15% modeled NPT reduction opportunity  
- ≥25% planning time reclaimed  
- ≥3 reusable playbooks

## Commercials
- Flat PoC fee **or** savings share option.
